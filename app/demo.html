Description

The wind turbine M5000-116 is a production of AREVA Wind GmbH, a manufacturer from Germany. This manufacturer has been in business since 2000.

The rated power of AREVA M5000-116 is 5,00 MW. At a wind speed of 4 m/s, the wind turbine starts its work. the cut-out wind speed is 25 m/s.

The rotor diameter of the AREVA M5000-116 is 116 m. The rotor area amounts to 10.568 m². The wind turbine is equipped with 3 rotor blades. The maximum rotor speed is 14,8 U/min.

The AREVA M5000-116 is fittet with a step-planetary gear. helical gearbox. Manufacturer of the transmission is Renk.

In the generator, AREVA Wind GmbH sets to Synchronous permanent. The manufacturer has used one generator for the M5000-116. The maximum speed of the generator is 148,5 U/min. The voltage amounts to 3.300 V. At the mains frequency, the M5000-116 is at 50 Hz.

In the construction of the tower, the manufacturer uses Steel tube. As corrosion protection for the tower AREVA focuses on painted.

We have 44 pictures of this wind turbine. There are no models for this wind turbine. Power data for the M5000-116 of AREVA are not stored in the system.

The AREVA M5000-116 has been listed since 24.08.2011. The last modification of the master data was made on 17.12.2018.
